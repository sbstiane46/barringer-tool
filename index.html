Abouts Us

It all started in a spare room of a college apartment. Under the mentorship of a master knifemaker, the first design was created. Years of process refinement brought limited numbers of the early models to market. After a decade of manufacturing engineering experience in the automotive, medical, and aerospace fields, we're back with a vengeance. It's the embodiment of the old adage "had I known then what I know now".

We strive to make robust tools for hard use; you won't find any safe queens here. Some makers focus on aesthetics, producing functional art of sorts. Not us. The primary focus is, and always will be, function. Form is a close second.
